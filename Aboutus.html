History

VM SteelTech is one of the largest pre-engineered steel building (PEB) companies in India. In 2003, Director of VM STEELTECH Mr. Vinay Mangla built the first logistics warehouse in the state of Haryana. Since then VM STEELLTECH has built its self owned 1 million sqft area along with several buildings for its clients all around India. VM Steeltech is a sister concern of one of the biggest Transportation company in Gurgaon Haryana ,India Known as Baroda Freight Carrier. VM SteelTech has a total of 2 manufacturing facilities located in Gurgaon, India, with a total capacity of about 100,000 MT per annum supported by over 100 employees. The company operates 7 sales offices located across India. VM SteelTech has more than 30 certified builders for providing erection of steel buildings along with other trades up to turnkey projects. With the vision of growing and building India , VM Steeltech now plans to expand its roots nationally and internationally in the future.

We believe that quality steel buildings conforming to Indian Standards and international codes of Practices sold at a fair price, with special emphasis on sound structural engineering, durability, quality fabrication, beautiful detailing and quick timely delivery suiting fast track projects, would ensure widespread customer acceptance.

Mission &  Vision

Mission : To provide precision engineering and technical products and solutions that are responsive to our client’s needs in an innovative and cost-effective manner.

Vision : To be recognized as the global leader for the design, manufacturing, supply and erection of Pre-Engineered Steel Buildings (PEB) and Structures.
